Volunteer Experience

I served as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in England for 18 months. My assignment included giving tours of a chapel in London and serving in the teaching center. At the teaching center, we received requests from people around the world who wanted to attend church in their local areas. We helped them connect with missionaries near them and taught them about our beliefs over the phone. Talking to people from many different countries and serving with missionaries from diverse backgrounds helped me develop strong communication skills and taught me how to work in unity with others. It was a valuable experience that pushed me out of my comfort zone and helped me grow both in my strengths and weaknesses.
