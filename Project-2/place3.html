Osu Shotengai Shopping Street

Shotengai means shopping street. Osu Shotengai connects Osu underground station to Kamimaezu underground station. It is usually clouded and has variety of shops including food trucks, vintage clothing stores, and arcades, so it's a little bit catotic, it's always fun to explore unique stores, and you can get pretty much all the trendy food.
