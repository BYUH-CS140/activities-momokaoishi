Nagoya City Science Museum

Nagoya has got a lot of high-quality museums, such as Nagoya City Art Museum, Port of Nagoya Public Aquarium, and Higashiyama Zoo and Botanical Gardens. One of museums that you should not miss is Nagoya City Science Museum. It is famous for the largest planetarium in the world. There are a variety of exhibition that both children and adult enjoy.
