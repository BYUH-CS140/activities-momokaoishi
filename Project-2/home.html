Photo Tour of Nagoya

Welcome to a photo tour of Nagoya, which is a city where I grew up. Nagoya is one of the big cities in Japan, located between Tokyo and Osaka. Nagoya might not be the most popular place to visit in Japan, however, it has a lot to see and eat to explore the uniqueness of Japan. Since Nagoya is easy to access other places in Japan, such as Tokyo and Osaka, it may be another place to stop next time you visit Japan!

Here are the best places to visit in Nagoya:

  • Oasis 21
  • Nagoya City Science Museum
  • Osu Shotengai Shopping Street
  • Misokatsu
