French Country Loaf

Bread is a baked food product made from water, flour, and yeast and is a staple food across the world particularly in Europe and the Middle East. Throughout recorded history and around the world, it has been an important part of many cultures' diets. It is one of the oldest human-made foods, having been of significance since the dawn of agriculture, and plays an essential role in both religious rituals and secular culture.

Ingredients (1 loaf)

  • 450g Cake flour
  • 30g Granulated sugar
  • 15g Salt
  • 7g Instant dry yeast
  • 250g Water

Instructions

  1. Put the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add the water.
  3. Mix until soft, smooth and elastic.
  4. Prove in a bowl covered in plastic until double in size.
  5. Weigh, shape and place on greased tray and dust with flour.
  6. Cover and prove until double in size.
  7. Dust again and score with sharp blade.
  8. Bake for 20-30mins at 220Â°C until dark brown.
